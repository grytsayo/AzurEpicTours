What to See in Cannes

From film festival glamour to medieval charm, Cannes offers unforgettable experiences

🎬

Palais des Festivals

Walk the famous red carpet where celebrities shine each May. Explore the Allée des Étoiles with handprints of film legends.

🌴

La Croisette

Stroll the legendary 2km boulevard lined with luxury hotels (Carlton, Martinez), designer boutiques, and palm trees facing golden beaches.

🏰

Le Suquet Old Town

Climb the historic hilltop quarter with cobbled streets, authentic bistros, and panoramic views over the bay and Lérins Islands.

🏖️

Beaches

Relax on sandy Mediterranean beaches. Choose from public beaches or exclusive beach clubs with sun loungers and restaurants.

⛵

Old Port (Vieux Port)

Browse the charming harbor with fishing boats, local market (Marché Forville), and seafood restaurants serving fresh catch.

🛍️

Luxury Shopping

Shop international designer brands on Rue d'Antibes and La Croisette: Chanel, Dior, Louis Vuitton, Hermès, and more.
