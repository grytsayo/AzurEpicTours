✨ About the Tour

The charm of the French Riviera extends beyond the famous beauties of Nice, Monaco and Cannes. Let me take you on a fascinating journey through the region's hidden gems.

Stunning view of the Bay of Angels and Nice hills

Medieval village on a 400-meter cliff

3. Fragonard Perfumery (45 min)

Fragrant oasis

Palace, cathedral, museum, casino, Formula 1 track

Picturesque artists' village

6. Antibes (1 hour)

Medieval charm on a rocky cliff

7. Cannes (1 hour)

Palais des Festivals, Croisette promenade
